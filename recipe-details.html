Kabuli Pulao

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Lamb
  • Carrots
  • Raisins
  • Spices

Instructions:

  1. Cook lamb with spices.
  2. Fry carrots and raisins.
  3. Combine rice and meat, then cook together.

Gosh-e-Fil (Sweet Pastry)

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Egg
  • Cardamom
  • Rosewater
  • Oil for frying
  • Powdered sugar

Instructions:

  1. Mix ingredients to make dough.
  2. Shape into elephant ear forms.
  3. Fry until golden and sprinkle sugar.

Doogh (Yogurt Drink)

Ingredients:

  • Yogurt
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Mint
  • Ice (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Mix yogurt and water well.
  2. Add salt and chopped mint.
  3. Serve chilled with ice.

Egg Flatbread (Quick Afghan Meal)

Ingredients:

  • Afghan bread
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat bread in pan.
  2. Add beaten eggs on top.
  3. Fry both sides and serve.
